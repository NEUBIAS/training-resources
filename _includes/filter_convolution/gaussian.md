<h4 id="gaussian"><a href="#gaussian">Gaussian filter</a></h4>

Gaussian blur ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaussian_blur)) is the most commonly used image smoothing filter. In this activity you will learn how to apply a Gaussian blur filter to an image.

Example image: [xy_8bit__spots_local_background_with_noise.tif](https://github.com/NEUBIAS/training-resources/raw/master/image_data/xy_8bit__spots_local_background_with_noise.tif)
