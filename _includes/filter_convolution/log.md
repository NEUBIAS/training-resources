<h4 id="log"><a href="#log">Laplacian of Gaussian filter</a></h4>

The Laplacian of Gaussian filter ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blob_detection#The_Laplacian_of_Gaussian)) is a relatively advanced convolutional filter that enhances edges without creating too much noise. 

In this activity you will learn how to create and apply a Laplacian of Gaussian filter to an image.

Example image: [xy_8bit__spots_local_background_with_noise.tif](https://github.com/NEUBIAS/training-resources/raw/master/image_data/xy_8bit__spots_local_background_with_noise.tif)
