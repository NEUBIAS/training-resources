<h4 id="laplacian"><a href="#laplacian">Laplacian filter</a></h4>

The Laplacian edge filter ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laplace_operator#Image_processing)) is a very basic "edge enhancement" filter. 

Observe that this filter mostly enhances spots, then ridges, then edges, while areas of equal intensity will be surpressed.

Example image: [xy_8bit__laplacian_test_input.tif](https://github.com/NEUBIAS/training-resources/raw/master/image_data/xy_8bit__laplacian_test_input.tif)
